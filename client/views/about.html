HOW IT GOT STARTED
The game within the game.
CoverSharks was founded by a group of college football fans who have been playing a fantasy sports game call "Pick Six" for over a decade. As an alum and avid fan of the Crimson Tide, Bill Wilder first created the original concept behind the game app in 2006. The idea was simple...give friends and college football fans alike the opportunity to compete in their favorite sport using an innovative format of game play that engages them in a unique, "game within the game" competition and enhances the overall entertainment experience.

THE COVERSHARKS TEAM
Over the past ten years, the players competing in the game have become part of a focus group that has seen vast potential in developing this unique format for play on a national scale. The number of players has steadily grown where the demand now exceeds the limits of the game. The game concept integrated with mobile app technology will make it available to sports fans across the country.
